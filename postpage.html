Here are some reasons pineapple is good for dogs.

High in fiber

Pineapple is high in fiber, which can help regulate a dogs digestive system and prevent constipation.

Aids digestion

Pineapple contains an enzyme called bromelain, which can aid your dogs digestion ny breaking down proteins in the stomach.

Rich in vitamins and minerals

Pineapple is a good source of vitamin C, vitamin B6, thiamin and potassium . These nutrients are important for maintaining overall health and wellness in dogs.
