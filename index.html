Can dogs eat pineapple?

Pineapple can provide many health benefits for dogs, including aiding digestion, regulating the digestive system and providing important vitamins and minerals.

As with any new food, its important to introduce pineapple gradually and in moderation. Ask your vet about giving your dog pineapple before you make any major changes to your dogs diet.

When feeding your dog pineapple, its important to remember that pineapple should only make up a small portion of your dogs diet. Too much pineapple can cause digestion upset and diarrhea. Additionally you should always remove the tough skin and remove the core, which can be difficult for dogs to digest.

Pineapple is a delicious and nutritious fruit that can provide many health benefits for dogs.
